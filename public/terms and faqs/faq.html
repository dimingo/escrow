What is SupaMall Escrow

SupaMall Escrow is a service available to SupaMall users that facilitates transactions between users on supamall.shop website. We aim to make transactions safe and secure while providing full transparency to both users. This is done by holding funds from a buyer in trust until the transaction is complete before releasing them to the seller. This provides security for both buyer and sellers on SupaMall.

How does SupaMall Escrow work?.

The following is a summary on how SupaMall Escrow works;
1. A user identifies a listing with the desired item on www.supamall.shop. The user then contacts the vendor as they agree on the terms of the transaction.
2. The Vendor initiates the transaction on SupaMall Escrow by filling in the terms of the transaction as agreed with the buyer.
3. The Buyer views the transaction that is created on SupaMall Escrow. If the details are correctly specified, the Buyer validates the transaction by accepting the terms. The Buyer then receives an Mpesa prompt to deposit the required funds into the SupaMall Escrow Paybill.
4. Once the funds are deposited, the Vendor receives a notification to begin the delivery of the required item to the Buyer.
5. We hold the funds on the SupaMall Escrow Paybill as the item is delivered to the buyer.
6. When the Buyer accepts delivery of the item, we automatically release the funds to the seller. If the seller rejects the item, they are refunded upon providing a legitimate reason for rejection.

How do I benefit from using SupaMall Escrow?

SupaMall Escrow creates a layer of trust and protection for both buyers and sellers. The buyer and seller are assured that neither party will take advantage of the other in a transaction. A buyer is protected because their funds are only released to the seller if they accept delivery of the item. When the buyer rejects the item, their funds are returned to them. On the other hand, a seller is protected because the buyer is required to deposit their funds into SupaMall Escrow before the transaction can begin. The buyer has no access to the funds as the transaction takes place. The funds are automatically released to the seller when the buyer accepts delivery of the item. This ensures that both buyers and sellers remain protected for the duration of the transaction. The buyer only pays for what they want, and the seller is guaranteed to receive their funds when the buyer accepts delivery of the item. We protect your product. We protect your cash.

How do I access SupaMall Escrow?

SupaMall Escrow is a service that is only available to registered SupaMall users. Consider registering as a user for free on SupaMall at www.supamall.shop to access SupaMall Escrow service.

How do I deposit funds onto SupaMall Escrow?

Once a transaction is initiated on SupaMall Escrow, the Buyer views and validates the transaction. Upon acceptance of the transaction, the Buyer receives an Mpesa push notification that prompts them to deposit funds to SupaMall Escrow Paybill. Alternatively, funds can also be deposited manually to our Mpesa Paybill

What if I receive the wrong item? What if the item arrives late

If a buyer receives an item that is different from what was agreed with the seller, we provide an option to reject the item on delivery. The buyer receives their money back immediately after they provide a valid reason for rejecting the item. The buyer may also choose to contact the seller when the product is first delivered and request for an exchange before the transaction is either accepted or rejected. Once the desired product is delivered, the buyer can then accept delivery for the transaction to be completed. If the item arrives later than the agreed delivery period, the buyer may choose to reject the item on delivery and trigger the reversal process. Users are only allowed to reject delivery of an item THREE times per month. We advise our users to ensure that there is complete agreement between the buyer and seller on the item before they initiate transactions on SupaMall Escrow.
